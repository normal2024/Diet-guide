The Seeds of Square Roots

Founded in 2016, Square Roots sprouted from a vision to bring fresh, healthy food to urban areas year-round. But it's not your typical farm. Instead, it's a technology-driven ecosystem that combines cutting-edge science, sustainable practices, and a dash of entrepreneurial spirit.

Beyond the Greenhouse

Square Roots isn't just about lettuce and tomatoes. It's a hub for agricultural research and development. Here's how they're branching out:

  1. Food Suppliers: Imagine grocery stores stocked with vibrant greens, regardless of the season. Square Roots partners with food suppliers, ensuring a consistent supply of fresh produce. Sustainability meets taste, and customers get a story worth sharing.
  2. Food R&D: Researchers and developers flock to Square Roots' indoor farms. Here, they explore plant behaviours, test novel approaches, and launch innovations. It's like a laboratory where kale meets curiosity.
  3. Healthier Harvests: Square Roots goes beyond calories and crunch. They focus on maximising beneficial compounds in plants—nutrients that boost human and animal health. It's not just about feeding bodies; it's about nourishing lives.
