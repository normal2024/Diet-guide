Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry (Vegan&GF)

Ingredients

  • Firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • Broccoli florets
  • Bell peppers (any color), thinly sliced
  • Snap peas, ends trimmed
  • Carrots, julienned or thinly sliced
  • Gluten-free tamari or soy sauce
  • Fresh ginger, grated
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Sesame oil or any vegetable oil for stir-frying
  • Cooked rice or quinoa (to serve)

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu to remove excess water and cut it into cubes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix 3 tablespoons of gluten-free tamari or soy sauce with grated ginger and minced garlic. Set aside.
  3. Heat a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add a tablespoon of sesame oil or vegetable oil.
  4. Add tofu cubes to the hot oil and stir-fry until they become golden and slightly crispy. Remove tofu from the wok and set aside.
  5. In the same wok, add a bit more oil if needed. Add broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots. Stir-fry the vegetables until they are crisp-tender but still vibrant.
  6. Return the cooked tofu to the wok with the vegetables.
  7. Pour the ginger-soy sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Stir well to coat everything evenly. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary. You can add a bit more tamari or soy sauce for extra flavor.
  9. Serve the tofu and vegetable stir-fry over cooked rice or quinoa.
  10. Garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.
Enjoy your Vegan and Gluten-Free Tofu and Vegetable Stir-Fry!
