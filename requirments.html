Nutritious Requirments

According to the British Nutrition Foundation, a male who is between the ages of 25 and 40, should be eating(per day):

  • Energy- Around 2500kcal
  • Fat- No more than 97g
  • Saturated fat- No more than 31g
  • Carbohydrate- At least 333g
  • Free Sugars- No more than 33g
  • Protein- 55g
  • Fibre- 30g
  • Salt- No more than 6g
