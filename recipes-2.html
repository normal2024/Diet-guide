Grilled Lemon Garlic Salmon with Quinoa

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Quinoa
  • Fresh lemon
  • Garlic cloves (minced)
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh dill (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Mixed vegetables (e.g., asparagus, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers)

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions. Set aside.
  2. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  3. In a small bowl, mix minced garlic, the juice of one lemon, chopped dill, and a tablespoon of olive oil to create the marinade.
  4. Pat the salmon fillets dry and brush both sides with the lemon garlic marinade. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Place the salmon fillets on the preheated grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side or until the salmon easily flakes with a fork.
  6. While the salmon is grilling, toss your preferred mixed vegetables with a bit of olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  7. Grill the mixed vegetables until they are slightly charred and tender.
  8. Serve the grilled salmon on a bed of cooked quinoa, surrounded by the grilled vegetables.
  9. Garnish with additional fresh dill and lemon wedges.
Enjoy your Grilled Lemon Garlic Salmon with Quinoa!
