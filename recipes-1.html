Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Olives
  • Olive oil dressing (extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, Mediterranean herbs)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and drain canned chickpeas thoroughly.
  2. Combine chickpeas, halved cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, finely chopped red onion, crumbled feta cheese, and sliced olives in a large bowl.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, and your choice of Mediterranean herbs (such as oregano or basil).
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Allow the flavors to meld by refrigerating the salad for at least 30 minutes before serving.
Enjoy your Mediterranean Chickpea Salad!
